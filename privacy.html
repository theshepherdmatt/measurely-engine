Privacy Policy

Measurely  ·  Last updated: March 2026

The short version

We only use your email address to save and sync your room data across devices. We do not sell your data, show you ads, or share it with third parties.

What we collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your email address — used solely as your account identifier
  • Your name and profile photo (if provided by Google) — displayed in the app header

We also store the data you enter into the app:

  • Room dimensions and speaker placement settings
  • Acoustic treatment configuration
  • REW measurement results and your notes

How we use it

  • To save your room profile and sync it across your devices
  • To display your name and avatar in the app
  • To associate your measurements with your account so you can retrieve them later

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics profiling, or any purpose other than making the app work for you.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data to anyone
  • We do not share your data with third parties
  • We do not use your email to send marketing emails
  • We do not track you across other websites

Google Sign-In

Measurely uses Google OAuth2 for authentication. When you click "Sign in with Google", Google authenticates you and shares your email, name, and profile photo with us. This process is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

We request the minimum OAuth2 scopes needed — only your basic profile and email address.

Data storage

Your room data is stored in PocketBase, a self-hosted backend. Data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Some data (such as your room settings) is also stored locally in your browser via localStorage for offline access.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or want to request deletion of your data, email us at info@measurely.uk.
