What Measurely tells you

graphic_eq

Peaks & Dips

Where your room is boosting or cancelling specific frequencies — the most common culprit behind dull or boomy bass.

blur_on

Reflections

Early reflections that smear your stereo image and make it hard to place instruments. Scored and explained.

tune

Balance

Whether bass, mids, and treble are in proportion, or whether your room is pushing one range too hard.

waves

Smoothness

How consistent your frequency response is across the audible range. Rougher = more coloration.

speaker

Bandwidth

How much of the frequency range your room is actually letting through to your ears.

psychology

Co-Pilot commentary

Plain-English explanations of what each score means for your room, and what to try first.
